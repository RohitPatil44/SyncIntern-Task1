Asus TUF Gaming F15

Product Description

The ASUS TUF Gaming F15 i5 11400H RTX 2050 is a 15.6-inch gaming laptop that is powered by an Intel Core i5-11400H processor and an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2050 GPU. It has 8GB of DDR4 RAM and a 512GB PCIe SSD. The laptop has a 144Hz FHD display, a backlit keyboard with RGB lighting, and a variety of ports, including USB 3.2 Gen 1, USB 3.2 Gen 2, HDMI, and an SD card reader. It runs Windows 11 Home.

The ASUS TUF Gaming F15 i5 11400H RTX 2050 is a great gaming laptop for those who are looking for a powerful and durable machine. It can handle most modern games at high settings, and it has a long battery life so you can game for hours on a single charge. The laptop is also MIL-STD-810H certified, which means that it can withstand accidental drops and bumps.
